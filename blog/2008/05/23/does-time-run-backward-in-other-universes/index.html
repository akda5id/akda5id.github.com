Does Time Run Backward in Other Universes?

May 23rd, 2008

A different theory than the earlier posted Loop Quantum Gravity (“quantum bounce”), though this article does touch on that idea in it’s sum up of previous research. Sean M. Carroll in Scientific American Magazine:
In the presence of dark energy, empty space is not completely empty.
